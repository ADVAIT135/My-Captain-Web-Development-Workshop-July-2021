About Us

New Edge Programmers college of technology(NEPCT) is a college which has a different base and concept of teaching programming to students. NEPCT is a first of it's kind college in India, in which it does not considers the performance of students of the basis of competitve exams like JEE,CET,etc. NEPCT has it's own testing system for the selection of student's. In the the testing system of NEPCT the students are asked questions that test their problem solving capabalities and also the basics of programming. The students go through a variety of range of questions of problem solving questions related to programming in the form of 100 MCQs and a coding problem at the end of the Student Selection System Test(SSST).
